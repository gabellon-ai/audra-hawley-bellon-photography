About Audra

I photograph the in-between moments — the real ones

I'm Audra Hawley Bellon, a 30A-based photographer who fell in love with this stretch of coast and never left. I've spent over a decade learning how the light moves here — how it turns the water jade at dusk, and how it catches your daughter's hair right before she laughs.

My sessions are unhurried, relaxed, and built around the way your family actually is together — not a stiff pose but a walk, a story, a stolen glance. My approach blends fine-art portraiture with the warmth of documentary storytelling.

Whether you're here for one week a year or have made Rosemary Beach your forever home, I want to give you images you'll frame, share, and return to for decades.

How It Works

Simple. Personal. Memorable.

From first message to finished gallery, the experience is designed to feel effortless — and the images are designed to last a lifetime.

01
Reach Out

Send a quick inquiry with your dates, location preference, and session type. I'll respond within 24 hours to check availability.

02
Planning Call

We'll talk through outfit guidance, locations, timing, and the vibe you're going for. This is also where I learn your family's story.

03
Golden Hour Session

We meet at the chosen location as the light softens. I guide gently while keeping things natural — no forced smiles, just real moments.

04
Gallery Delivery

Your curated gallery of fully edited, high-resolution images is delivered within 3 weeks via private online gallery — ready to download, print, and keep forever.
